Das Blinds

For this project I wanted to turn some Ikea blinds into remote controlled blinds. I used a NodeMCU as the brains of the project in conjunction with some other parts I had laying around from previous projects or from friends who were no longer using them. This time my friend gave me a sparkfun inventor kit that included a geared DC motor and a motor driver. I did have to buy some blinds from Ikea, an 18650 battery holder from ebay. I 3D printed a motor housing and a gear to connect the motor to the blinds. The NodeMCU is running a websever which controls the 'up' 'down' and 'stop' controls.
