Solar Charger Project

With all of the recent power outages that California has faced I decided it would be a good idea to build a small solar charger/backup to power things around the house incase of an emergency. Recently I have also built a small shed that does not have power coming into it so I thought it would be a good idea to run some 12V lights to use over the work bench inside the shed for some extra lighting.

For this project I have sourced a scratched 50W solar panel from an online vender, a cheap PWM charge controller from amazon, a set of 12V lights for an RV, a recycled sealed lead acid battery (I believe this was sourced from a broken electric scooter).
Currently I have the the charge controller connected to a 12V cigarette outlet which is connected to a 2 USB buckboost converter to charge battery packs for my phone and laptop. I also have a Nitecore charger that I can charge AA/AAA/18650 batteries I have around the house
