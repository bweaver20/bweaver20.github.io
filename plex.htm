Linux Plex Server

I decided to repurpose an old desktop I had laying around from circa 2006. The spec's are nothing special:

    AMD Athlon 64X2
    3GB DDR2
    400GB HDD

Originally I planned to buy the Rasperry Pi 4 with 4GB of ram but decided instead of spending the money on the Pi I could buy a 2TB HDD to put in the desktop and have a faster machine. The trade off of using such an old desktop compared to a tiny Pi is the power consumption and size. I decided to remove anything I didn't need from the MoBo... Good bye optical drive, multi card reader and hello Ubuntu. The computer ran fine on windows 7 but figured I might as well do a fresh install of ubuntu because why not?

    Components needed:

    Working Computer
    4GB USB Drive(Ubuntu)
    Storage Drive
