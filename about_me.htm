About Me

My name is Brandon and I am currently a senior (May 2020 Graduation) at San Francisco State University studying electrical engineering. I've decided to create a website to post some of the projects I am working on.
I like taking old electronics that most people could consider to be trash and give them new life. We live in a time and place where a lot of times it is easier to just throw things away and buy something new. However I think things should be serviceable and if I can take some "trash" and turn it into something that works why not bring it back to life and get some more use of it
